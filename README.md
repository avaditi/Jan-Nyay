⚖️ Jan-Nyay – AI Legal Copilot

📌 Overview

Jan-Nyay is an AI-powered legal assistance platform designed to make legal documents understandable for everyone.

Users can upload contracts, agreements, insurance policies, government notices, and other legal documents to receive simplified summaries, risk analysis, fairness evaluation, and actionable legal insights in plain language.

Our mission is to make justice more accessible, transparent, and affordable for every citizen.

🚀 Features

📄 Document Analysis

Upload legal documents instantly.

AI extracts key clauses and important information.

Generates easy-to-understand summaries.

⚠️ Risk Radar

Detects risky or unfair clauses.

Highlights:

Hidden charges

Penalties

Liability issues

Auto-renewal clauses

Contractual obligations

⚖️ Fairness Score

Evaluates how balanced an agreement is.

Compares rights, obligations, penalties, and termination conditions.

Generates an overall fairness rating.	

🔍 What-If Simulator	

Simulates real-world legal scenarios.

Helps users understand possible consequences before taking action.

🌐 Multi-Language Support

Provides legal insights in regional languages.

Makes legal information accessible to a wider audience.

🎯 Problem Statement

Legal documents are often difficult for ordinary citizens to understand because of complex legal language, hidden clauses, and lengthy agreements.

This creates confusion, increases legal risks, and limits access to justice.

💡 Solution

Jan-Nyay simplifies legal documents using Artificial Intelligence by:

Explaining legal jargon in plain language

Identifying risks and unfair clauses

Providing fairness analysis

Educating citizens about their rights

Helping users make informed legal decisions
